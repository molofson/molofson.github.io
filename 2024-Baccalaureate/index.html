• 2024 Elk River High School Baccalaureate

    Zabee Theatre - Elk River High School - Sunday, June 2nd at 7PM

  • A baccalaureate is a farewell celebration in the form of a Christian service honoring all 2024 graduates of Elk River High School.

    In its 9th year, this ceremony has become a highly attended graduation tradition for the Elk River community. It is open to all Elk River High School graduating seniors and their families.

    This celebration is planned by a committee of parents with the support of local churches. We will celebrate your completion of high school and encourage you as you venture out after graduation.

    Please register below by May 19th for planning and facilities purposes.

    As part of registration, you may optionally submit two photos of your graduate to be included in the event slideshow: one childhood photo and one senior photo. If you are not yet ready to submit photos, please continue to register. You can update your submission to add update any details using the "edit" link in the email sent to you after successfully completing registration. 

    Graduates, please wear your cap and gown at the event.

    Join the Facebook group for up-to-date information and to connect and inform your graduate’s friends and family members

    Email questions to the planning committee (info@elkriversenior.com).

  • There is no cost to attend this event.

    The Baccalaureate Committee also is providing two $500 scholarships from the offering collected during the previous years' baccalaureate service. This is just two of over 600 scholarships available from ISD728.

    The scholarship application is available at the Elk River High School website beginning March 1st and the deadline for application completion is March 15th. Scholarships are awarded at Awards Evening on May 17th.

    Although we are providing these two specific scholarships, questions should be directed to Elk River High School administration, as they are coordinating the scholarship awareness effort.
